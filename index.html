About Me

When I decided that I wanted to become a web developer, apart of me thought that I was out of my mind. I had no reason to believe that this was the right thing for me to do, and I couldn't quite put my finger on my sudden desire to make the shift. It wasn't until I looked at my first finished product (a weakly developed flashcard game) that I felt something inside of me. The feeling felt comparable to the sensation of a match striking itself against that of the matchbox, a seemingly simple action created something much stronger. A fire inside of me.

As a developer, I embody the spirit of persistence and service as I strive to create applications that provide peak functionality and value to users, companies, and clients alike. Feel free to take a look at my work to see what I have to offer! I specialize in front-end development, but I possess the capabilities to manipulate back-end frameworks as well. I desire to learn, I desire to become better in everyway that I can. That is my goal.

When I am not glued to my computer, I enjoy taking scenic runs through the Rock Creek Park, revisiting my childhood by playing Nintendo64 games, and reading.

I am currently seeking an amazing role as a front-end web developer and I would love to chat about a new opportunity. Feel free to reach out!
